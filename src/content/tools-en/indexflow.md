IndexFlow addresses a growing challenge for enterprises: as businesses scale, unstructured documents (contracts, reports, emails, research materials) multiply rapidly, and traditional search and filing methods can no longer keep up.

The platform's core capability is AI auto-indexing — upload documents and the system automatically identifies document types, extracts key information (dates, parties, clauses), and builds a semantic search index. Users can ask questions in natural language and the system pinpoints relevant content across tens of thousands of documents.

**Industries served:** Legal (contract management, case document retrieval), Finance (regulatory compliance, audit reports), Research (literature management, knowledge bases), and Government (archive digitization and intelligent retrieval).
