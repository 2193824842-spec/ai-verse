Free AI Video Upscaler is based on the Upscayl open-source project and is one of the most popular free video upscaling tools available. It brings AI upscaling capabilities — previously only available in professional software — to everyday users, with no subscription, no internet connection required, and fully local processing.

The tool includes Real-ESRGAN, Anime4K, BSRGAN, and other mainstream upscaling models, capable of upscaling standard-definition video to 2K, 4K, or higher while preserving detail and reducing noise. For anime content, the Anime4K model delivers exceptional results.

**Best for:** Video editors and content creators, archiving and restoring old footage, anime enthusiasts, and technically comfortable desktop users.
