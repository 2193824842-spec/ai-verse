Sentence 3.0 is a next-generation AI writing assistant designed to help marketing teams and content creators break through productivity bottlenecks. The platform covers everything from short-form copy (ad headlines, product descriptions, social posts) to long-form content (blog articles, whitepapers, email sequences), with a built-in template library for different industries and use cases.
